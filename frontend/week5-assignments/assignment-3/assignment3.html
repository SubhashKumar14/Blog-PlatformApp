MITTI CAFÉ

The Café

Mitti Café is a socially conscious café empowering individuals with disabilities by providing employment and skill-building opportunities. We serve healthy, homemade meals, snacks, and beverages in a warm and welcoming environment.

Catering

Enjoy your event while we take care of the food. Mitti Café offers catering services for corporate events, community gatherings, and special occasions with fresh and nutritious meals.
